Chris Bumstead

The man who set a new Standard.

Here's a time line of CBUM's Life :

  • 2 February 1995 - born in Ottawa , Canada
  • 2015 - started competing in bodybuilding
  • 2016 - became a pro bodybuilder
  • 2019 - Cbum won his first Mr. Olympia Classic Physique title, defeating Breon Ansley and marking the beginning of his reign in the category. This victory was significant despite him managing an autoimmune kidney disease.
  • 2020 - He defended his title at the 2020 Mr. Olympia, winning his second consecutive Classic Physique title. His health challenges continued, but he made notable improvements in his physique, further establishing his dominance.
  • 2021 - Cbum achieved his third Mr. Olympia Classic Physique win, bringing an even more refined physique and solidifying his legacy in the sport. This win showcased his dedication to improving each year.
  • 2022 - In 2022, Cbum secured his fourth consecutive Mr. Olympia Classic Physique title, becoming one of the most successful competitors in the category’s history. His influence grew as he continued setting new standards in aesthetics and balance.
  • 2023 - Cbum achieved his fifth Mr. Olympia Classic Physique title, setting a record and further establishing himself as an icon in bodybuilding. This year also marked his marriage to Courtney King, adding a significant personal milestone to his achievements.
  • 2024 - Cbum achieved his sixth Mr. Olympia Classic Physique title and announced his retirement to focus on family and business. With a daughter to care for, he chose to dedicate more time to his personal life and business ventures, balancing family and professional commitments.

In conclusion, Chris Bumstead’s six Mr. Olympia titles have cemented his legacy as a dominant force in Classic Physique. Beyond his impressive physique, his focus on balance and personal growth in retirement shows there’s more to his journey than just bodybuilding. He remains an inspiration to many.
